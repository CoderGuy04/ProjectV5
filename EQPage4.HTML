Different Parts

Next, we have the prefrontal cortex. In the image above, we can see that it's the little green color-coded area. This is considered part of your "upstairs brain", also known as the part that helps you think through things, although you need to be calm, otherwise you'll get stuck in your "downstairs brain". It works with the cerebral cortex to do this and many things you do when you don't feel like you're in danger.
